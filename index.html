All About Me: Gillian Coppola

An introduction to things that I love


My Friends and Family

My friends and family are super important in my life. They live all over the place and I can't always see them when I want to, but I love to spend time with them anyway.


Music

I love music and listen to it all the time--last year my Spotify minutes came out to almost 50,000. Music from the 60's and 70's is the best. Here's a list of my favorite artists.

  • Pink Floyd
  • The Beatles
  • Led Zeppelin
  • The Rolling Stones
  • Queen
  • Steve Vai

My Passions

(Art, writing, and guitar)

I am an art major, so of course I love art. I love drawing, painting, and sculpting, and my favorite thing to depict is people and faces.

I love to write stories about characters that I create. I am writing a book right now about a character who gets transported to another world and gets involved in its politics.

I play guitar every day. My favorite thing to play is rock music from the 70's. I also write my own songs all the time. I hope m,y band becomes famous.


The End. Thanks for reading my website!
